Carbin Reef

Carbin Reef is a huge white sandbar in Negros Occidental that looks like a tongue in shape. It is the center of attraction in the 32,000-hectare marine reserve where mangroves, corals and variety of marine species can be found. The white sandbar is surrounded with crystal clear waters perfect for swimming and snorkeling. For non-swimmers, the waters near the sandbar is just shallow giving you great opportunity to enjoy water sports/games. The sandbar can approximately accommodate a hundred people. Carbin Reef is located in Sagay City, Negros Occidental. Contact first the tourism office of Sagay City before you visit Carbin Reef.
