Buenos Aires Mountain Resort

Buenos Aires Mountain Resort is one of the best places in Negros Occidetal for the locals and tourists who wants to relax and cool down for a while. Feel the aura of nature as the resort was enclosed with trees and one can find a unique picture perfect garden. The resort is also dotted with picnic huts for visitors and excellent accommodations for those who wants to stay overnight. It also has a refreshing pools since the fresh and cold waters came from the mountain springs. Buenos Aires Mountain Resort is located in Barangay Ilijan, Bago City, Negros Occidental. Around 1 hour travel time from Bacolod City.
