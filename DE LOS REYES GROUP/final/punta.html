Punta Bulata White Beach Resort and Spa

One of the famous tourist spots in Negros Occidental is the Punta Bulata White Beach Resort and Spa. The beach resort is a hidden paradise with a kilometer-long stretch of fine white sand beach where you can enjoy in privacy and exclusivity especially when you stay at the resort. The staffs earned a good reputation being friendly and approachable. If you want more than just swimming and snorkeling, they can also arrange a day tour to Danjugan Island Marine Reserve. Punta Bulata White Beach Resort and Spa is located in Cauayan, Negros Occidental.
