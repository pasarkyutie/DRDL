Mag-Aso Falls

Mag-Aso Falls is an enchanting waterfalls in Negros Occidental famous of its turquoise or bluish water that makes its basin very attractive that lures visitors for a swim. Aside from the falls, there is also a man-made swimming pool fed by the free-flowing spring water that makes it very refreshing. Cottages are also available for day trip and rooms for overnight stay. Bring your group friends here and have a good time. The rates could possibly change but as of writing this, its Php 50 for the entrance, Php 200 for Nipa Hut, Php 1000 for the room. Mag-Aso Falls is located in Barangay Oringao, Kabankalan City, Negros Occidental.
